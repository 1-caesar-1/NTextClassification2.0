Before
I've been here before. The solitude and the silence are unnerving, but not unpleasant. The sounds of life come and go. and only certain sounds are annoying.
Everything suffered is useful, even if painful. It is useful for other people, for it is not meant to be gratifying, but purifying, and unifying in a sanctifying kind of way.
He may have an exasperating sense of humor, but I'd rather suffer that than His wrath. In the night I remember things I'd forgotten, and some of them make me cry, even to this day.
Sleep is granted, not imposed, and when scarce, one is grateful when it comes, but that is only realized after the fact.
We do not know we are dying usually, but sometimes it becomes clear that time is not far off.
Offer everything.
